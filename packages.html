Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our packages and services

Do you offer customized packages?

Yes, we understand that every business has unique requirements. While we offer standard packages to provide a starting point, we're happy to customize a solution that perfectly fits your needs and budget. Please contact us to discuss your specific requirements.


What is the typical timeline for project completion?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. Basic websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while more complex web applications or desktop software may take 8-12 weeks or longer. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed timeline based on your specific project requirements.


Do you provide ongoing support after the project is completed?

Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your software continues to run smoothly. Our support includes bug fixes, security updates, and technical assistance. We can discuss the appropriate support option for your needs during our consultation.


What is your payment structure?

We typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, we may establish a milestone-based payment schedule. We accept bank transfers and major credit cards.


Can you help migrate data from our existing systems?

Yes, we offer data migration services to help you transition from your existing systems to your new solution. Our team will work with you to ensure a smooth migration process with minimal disruption to your operations.
